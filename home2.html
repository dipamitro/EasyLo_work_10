Tailor-made business language training

The practical employability of your language skills is of great importance for business success. That is why our business language courses focus on the communication skills that are essential in your specific business situation.

We tailor our approach to each individual student or team. With us you learn to use the language that suits your own (learning) style, situation, position, sector, goal and wishes. You learn exactly what you need, focused on practice and without ballast. We have included specially developed modules in our course material for typical business conversation situations such as negotiating, meeting or presenting your products at a trade fair.
