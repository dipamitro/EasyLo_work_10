Training forms

Taleninstituut Nederland offers you various business language courses, depending on the number of employees, their entry level, the goal to be achieved and the available time. All our training courses are tailor-made, practical and fully tailored to the entry level of the student and the needs and wishes of the company.

We offer training at any level. From beginners who have no knowledge of the language they want to learn with us to employees who are already able to express themselves orally and in writing in this language; we draw up a suitable training program for everyone.

You can follow the language courses below at our centrally located training locations in Amsterdam , Rotterdam , The Hague and Utrecht , remotely or in-company. A combination of the training forms below is also welcome.

Flexible language training

Flexible training courses are particularly suitable for students who do not have much time available. Language courses of two to three hours are given at times to be agreed with you. More information

Semi-intensive language training

Semi-intensive language training means that the student attends a number of full days of training spread over a number of weeks. This form is recommended for students for whom flexible training is too slow and who can free up more time for their language training in certain periods. More information

Intensive language training

In the intensive language training, the course participant has full days of lessons for one or more weeks. This form is suitable for students who need to quickly raise their knowledge of a foreign language to a higher level and who are motivated to spend some time (almost) full-time with the language.More information

Remote language training

Distance language courses are suitable for individual students or groups who want to follow a course from home, work at different locations or are regularly on business trips.More information

E-learning system

In addition to our tailor-made programs, you can also use our e-learning system (in combination). You can use this program anywhere, anytime and at your own pace to deepen your knowledge of business language.More information
